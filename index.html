Answering Your Curiosities

Are these formulations structurally stable without synthetic parabens?

Yes, absolutely. We leverage standard-compliant plant-derived organic preservation matrices such as Radish Root Ferment Filtrate and Honeysuckle Extract. These guarantee structural product stability up to 12 months after breaking the bottle vacuum seal.

How long does it take to visually detect structural skin variations?

Dermal cellular turnover spans roughly 28 days. While deep cellular moisture increases inside 2 hours, expect marked superficial shifts in hyperpigmentation, collagen visibility, and elasticity within 4 to 6 weeks of regimented daily application.

Can I safely layer your high-potency Vitamin C with retinol?

We highly suggest separating these items. Apply our Vitamin C serum in your daytime routine to maximize environmental oxidation shield performance, and reserve heavy retinoid correctors exclusively for nighttime cellular repair periods to mitigate surface pH conflicts.

Is your glass packaging sustainably sourced globally?

100% of our production bottles utilize pure pharmaceutical-grade amber glass produced from 70% locally reclaimed cullet materials. This heavily diminishes logistical emission overhead and shields sensitive botanical ingredients from direct UV degradation.
